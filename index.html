About me

A student, a builder,
a curious mind.

My name is Sharanabasappa Patil — but everyone calls me Sharan (or Ash, if you've known me long enough). I'm a first-year BTech Computer Science student at Poojya Doddappa Appa College of Engineering, Kalaburagi.

I came into CS not because it was a "safe career choice," but because I couldn't stop wondering how things work. I started with C — raw, close to the metal — and I loved how it made me think about every byte and every loop.

"The best programs aren't just correct — they tell you something about the person who wrote them."

Right now I'm deep in the fundamentals: algorithms, pointers, recursion, and building small things that actually run. I've got a long way to go, and honestly, that's the most exciting part.
